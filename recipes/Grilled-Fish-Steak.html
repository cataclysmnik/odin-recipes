Grilled Fish Steak

Description

Tender fish fillets smeared in a simple marinade of lime juice and salt, seared golden. Delicious, isn't it? A super simple dish with refreshing summery flavours from the fresh herbs and a dash of chilli to spice it up.

  • Fish fillets
  • Lemon juice
  • Coriander powder
  • Ginger
  • Green chilli
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Salt
  • Red pepper flakes

Steps

  1. To prepare this amazing non-vegetarian recipe, take the fish fillets and massage it gently with oil, keep aside in a plate.
  2. Grind together the garlic, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, green chillies, dill leaves, coriander powder, and salt. Add oil to it and grind to form a paste. Rub this all over the fish fillets and keep aside to marinate for 15 to 30 minutes.
  3. Grill the marinated fish on a preheated grill or oven till golden and crisp on both sides or for 5 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
  4. Pour the lime juice and chilli flakes, and serve with chutney or salad of your choice. Make sure you rate this recipe, try it out and let us know in the comments how it turned out to be.
